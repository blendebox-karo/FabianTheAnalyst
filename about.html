About Me

Hi! I'm Anaro Karo Fabian, a data analyst with a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive business decisions. With a strong background in computing and statistics, data visualization, and problem-solving, I thrive on finding patterns in data that others might overlook.

I believe that data tells a story, and my job is to bring that story to life. Whether it’s building interactive dashboards, running predictive models, or cleaning up messy datasets, I aim to turn complexity into clarity. One of my favorite projects involved analyzing customer behavior data to boost conversion rates by 20%. A perfect example of how I use data to make a measurable impact.

With expertise in SQL and Python, I can efficiently manage and analyze complex datasets to extract valuable insights. Using SQL, I’m able to query, manipulate, and structure data across multiple databases, ensuring accurate and streamlined reporting. Python enhances my ability to automate data cleaning, perform statistical analysis, and build predictive models using libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn. Whether it’s writing efficient SQL queries to optimize database performance or developing Python scripts for in-depth data analysis, I bring a blend of technical skills and analytical thinking to solve your data challenges effectively.
